Paneer Butter Masala

Jump to Ingredients | Jump to Steps | Jump to Tips

Prep Time 10 mins
Cook Time 20 mins
Servings 3
Difficulty Medium

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices and salt
  • 2 tbsp cream

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan.
  2. Add onion and sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomato puree. Cook well.
  4. Add spices and salt.
  5. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add cream and mix well. Serve hot.

Tips

Use fresh paneer for the best soft texture.

Vegetable Pulao

Jump to Ingredients | Jump to Steps

Prep Time 15 mins
Cook Time 20 mins
Servings 4
Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion
  • Salt and oil

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and onion.
  3. Add vegetables and sauté.
  4. Add rice, water, and salt.
  5. Cook until rice is soft. Serve warm.

Aloo Paratha

Jump to Ingredients | Jump to Steps

Prep Time 20 mins
Cook Time 15 mins
Servings 3
Difficulty Medium

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour dough
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Spices
  • Butter or ghee

Steps

  1. Prepare dough using wheat flour and water.
  2. Mix potatoes with spices.
  3. Stuff potato mixture inside dough ball.
  4. Roll into paratha shape.
  5. Cook on tawa using butter/ghee. Serve hot.

Masoor Dal

Jump to Ingredients | Jump to Steps

Prep Time 10 mins
Cook Time 25 mins
Servings 4
Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup masoor dal
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • Cumin seeds
  • Salt and turmeric

Steps

  1. Wash and pressure cook dal with water and turmeric.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and onion.
  3. Add tomato and cook well.
  4. Add cooked dal and salt.
  5. Simmer for 5 minutes and serve.

Suji Halwa

Jump to Ingredients | Jump to Steps

Prep Time 5 mins
Cook Time 15 mins
Servings 4
Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup suji
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • Dry fruits

Steps

  1. Heat ghee and roast suji until aromatic.
  2. Boil water and add to suji carefully.
  3. Add sugar and mix continuously.
  4. Cook until thick.
  5. Add dry fruits and serve warm.
